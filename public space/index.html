About Hajj Platform

The Hajj Platform is a comprehensive digital system designed to facilitate and manage the Hajj pilgrimage process. Our platform provides a transparent, fair, and inclusive way for Muslims worldwide to participate in this sacred journey through a lottery system.

With our innovative technology, we ensure that every eligible individual has an equal opportunity to be selected for the upcoming Hajj seasons. Our platform maintains the highest standards of security, privacy, and fairness.

Sign Up Rules

  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old
  • Must possess a valid national identification number (NIN)
  • Must provide accurate personal and contact information
  • Must be physically and mentally able to perform Hajj
  • Must agree to the terms and conditions of the platform
  • Must not have been selected for Hajj in the previous 5 years
  • Current financial obligations must be manageable

Lottery Rules & Selection Process

  1. Random Selection: Winners are selected randomly from all eligible participants using a certified random draw mechanism.
  2. Fairness: All eligible applications have an equal chance of being selected.
  3. Lottery Schedule: Lotteries are conducted annually with specified start and end dates for registrations.
  4. Result Announcement: Winners are announced publicly on the results page after the lottery is concluded.
  5. Disqualification: Any fraudulent or misleading information will result in immediate disqualification and potential legal action.
  6. Appeal Process: Applicants may appeal their non-selection within 30 days if they believe there was an error.
